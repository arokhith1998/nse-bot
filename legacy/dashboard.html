How To Use This Nightly

  1. After market close (~16:00 IST): run python generate_picks.py in your terminal. It pulls the latest EOD data via yfinance, recomputes scores, and writes picks.json.
  2. Refresh this dashboard. The picks table updates from the freshest scores.
  3. Open Groww in paper-trading mode (or just watch only). At 09:15 IST, wait for the first 15-min candle to form before entering.
  4. Enter only if price respects the entry zone and bias still aligns with broader market open. Place SL immediately.
  5. Log every trade in the Journal above, even paper ones. After 30 trades you'll know whether the system has any edge.
  6. If win-rate < 40% and net P&L negative after 30 paper trades → the system has no edge. Do not graduate to real capital.

Methodology (so you know exactly what's under the hood)

Each watchlist stock is scored 0-100 from these signals: Trend (EMA20 vs EMA50), Momentum (RSI 14), Volume (today vs 20-day avg), Breakout (proximity to 20-day high/low), Volatility (ATR% — too low = no intraday range), Liquidity (turnover floor). Trade plan: SL = 1×ATR(14), Target = 2×ATR (2:1 R:R). Position sizing: qty = ⌊₹50 / (entry−SL)⌋, capped by ₹1000 capital. No look-ahead, no fundamentals override, no sentiment AI — all rules are inspectable in generate_picks.py.

What it deliberately ignores: options OI, FII/DII intraday flows, order book, social sentiment, earnings calendar (you should layer this manually), corporate actions. Treat output as a starting hypothesis, never a signal to act blindly on.
